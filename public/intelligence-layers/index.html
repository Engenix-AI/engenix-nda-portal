01In active development

Operating Command

What requires leadership attention across departments right now?

Operating Command is being developed as the leadership view across ENGENIX: unresolved operating decisions, accountable owners, deadlines and cross-department exposure in one management view.

Responsible operator: Dealer principal, general manager, executive leadership.

02Preparing for controlled deployment

Desking Intelligence

What structure gives the deal the strongest path forward?

Desking Intelligence is being prepared to preserve the customer objective, vehicle, trade position, lender pathway and approved economics surrounding the proposed structure.

Responsible operator: Desk manager, GSM, sales leadership.

03Preparing for controlled deployment

F&I Intelligence

What ownership strategy fits the customer, lender, vehicle and approved economics?

F&I Intelligence is being prepared to keep the approved structure visible while qualified dealership personnel determine the final ownership strategy.

Responsible operator: F&I manager, F&I director.

04 Operational today

Compliance Intelligence

Does the final transaction align with the record, disclosures, approvals and management decisions?

Compliance Intelligence helps qualified dealership personnel surface potential exposure, review evidence, assign corrective action and preserve decision history around the deal jacket.

Responsible operator: Compliance leadership, F&I leadership, general management.

05In active development

Funding Assurance

Is the packet consistent, complete and ready to fund?

Funding Assurance is being developed to surface packet inconsistency, missing requirements, accountable owners and unresolved conditions before they become longer funding delays.

Responsible operator: Funding owner, F&I leadership, accounting.

06In active development

Controller Intelligence

Did the dealership fund, book, accrue, pay and retain what the deal was expected to produce?

Controller Intelligence is being developed to support deal-to-ledger reconciliation, financial exposure and close readiness without replacing the DMS or general ledger.

Responsible operator: Controller, accounting leadership, ownership.

07Preparing for controlled deployment

Guided Experience

What does this individual employee need to do next?

Guided Experience is being prepared as the role-based usability layer that turns operating intelligence into clear priorities, supporting evidence and next moves.

Responsible operator: Every authorized ENGENIX user.
