One transaction

The deal changes hands. Its operating truth should not.

A transaction moves from customer objective and proposed structure through approval, presentation, contracting, verification, funding, booking and close. Every handoff inherits the decisions made before it.

Establish the strongest path forward around the customer objective, vehicle, trade position, lender pathway and approved economics.

03
