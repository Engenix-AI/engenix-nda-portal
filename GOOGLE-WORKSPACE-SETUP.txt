ENGENIX GOOGLE WORKSPACE FORM V13

1. Create a Google Sheet named ENGENIX Demo Leads.
2. Open Extensions → Apps Script.
3. Replace the starter code with google-apps-script/Code.gs.
4. Change CONFIG.NOTIFICATION_EMAIL to your chosen @engenix.co inbox.
5. Save, choose setupEngenix, click Run, and approve permissions.
6. Deploy → New deployment → Web app.
7. Execute as: Me.
8. Who has access: Anyone.
9. Deploy and copy the URL ending in /exec.
10. In website script.js, replace YOUR_GOOGLE_APPS_SCRIPT_WEB_APP_URL with that /exec URL.
11. Redeploy the website.
12. Test /beta/ and /beta/demo.html.

The system logs leads in Google Sheets, emails ENGENIX, sends a prospect confirmation, calculates lead score/priority, and includes spam/rate controls.

Use the /exec URL, not /dev. After later Code.gs edits, deploy a new version.
